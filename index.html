Why real estate workflows break most AI assistants

Speed‑to‑lead is unforgiving

Research cited across real estate AI platforms shows the first agent to respond wins the client roughly 78% of the time. Yet average response times stretch into hours or days once you are in showings or closings.

Listings live behind logins

MLS systems, showing schedulers, and CRMs require real clicks, forms, and authentication. Text‑only assistants can draft messages — they cannot actually do the work.

Repetition kills margins

Updating the same listing across portals, logging every follow‑up, and rescheduling showings costs time every single run if the agent does not remember what it already did.

Computer use changes the equation

As highlighted in coverage of Gemini’s new computer‑use capabilities, the market is moving from chatbots to agents that can actually operate browsers and desktops.
